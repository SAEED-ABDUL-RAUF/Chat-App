The requirements for creating the app

Campus-wide Communication: The app should facilitate real-time communication among students, faculty, and staff across different departments and campus locations.

Group Messaging: Users should be able to create and participate in group chats based on courses, clubs, events, or other interests, with options for multimedia sharing and message search functionality.

Privacy Controls: The app should offer privacy settings that allow users to control who can contact them, join their groups, and view their profile information.

Integration with Campus Services: Users should be able to access university services such as academic advising, library resources, and campus events directly through the chat app via chatbots or integration with existing systems.

Announcements and Alerts: The app should support announcements and alerts from university administration, departments, and student organizations, with options for users to customize their notification preferences.

User Profiles: Users should have customizable profiles where they can display their academic interests, extracurricular activities, and contact information, helping facilitate networking and collaboration within the university community.

Accessibility: The app should be designed to be accessible to users with disabilities, with features such as support for screen readers, high contrast modes, and alternative input methods.

Security: The app should prioritize user data security and privacy, with end-to-end encryption for messages, secure authentication mechanisms, and regular security audits.
